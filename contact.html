Travel Tips & Useful Links

These basic tips and example links help you plan a safe and enjoyable trip to Nepal. Always confirm details with official sources before you travel.

Basic Travel Tips

  • Documents: Check visa requirements and make sure your passport is valid long enough.
  • Health: Drink bottled or filtered water, and give your stomach time to adapt to new food.
  • Respect: Remove shoes in temples when requested, dress modestly and ask before taking close photos of people.
  • Money: Carry small notes of Nepalese Rupees. ATMs are common in big cities but not in remote areas.
  • Transport: Domestic flights save time; local buses are cheaper but slower and more crowded.

4-Day City & Culture Plan

  • Day 1: Arrive in Kathmandu, evening walk around Thamel or local area.
  • Day 2: Visit Swayambhu, Boudhanath and Pashupatinath.
  • Day 3: Day trip to Bhaktapur or Patan for heritage squares.
  • Day 4: Souvenir shopping and relaxed café time before departure.

7-Day Nepal Highlights

  • Day 1: Kathmandu sightseeing — visit Swayambhunath, Boudhanath and explore old squares.
  • Day 2: Drive or fly to Pokhara, enjoy a peaceful lakeside walk in the evening.
  • Day 3: Take a short hike or visit a nearby viewpoint for sunrise (Sarangkot or World Peace Pagoda).
  • Day 4: Free day in Pokhara — boating on Phewa Lake, museums, or optional paragliding.
  • Day 5: Travel to Chitwan National Park or another wildlife area.
  • Day 6: Join jungle activities — safari, canoe ride, bird watching or cultural program.
  • Day 7: Return to Kathmandu and prepare for departure or souvenir shopping.

Simple Cost Overview (Rough Idea)
